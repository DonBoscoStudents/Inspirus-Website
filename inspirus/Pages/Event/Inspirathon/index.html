Eligibility

  • Students from Undergraduate, Diploma ,professional and non-professional institutions.
  • Student Should possess A Valid Institute ID
  • Team members have to be a current student
  • Max 3 Team Members
  • Team members of each team Can be from different colleges (Interdepartmental teams are allowed)

Rules

  • Teams can use any programming language or software of their choice.
  • Teams have to carry their own laptops/hardware.
  • Online Registrations will close On the 2th of October at 5:00 PM OR Max 15 Teams
  • Problem statements will be released 48 Hrs Before the event on 4th October at 11:00 AM. All the statements will be sent to the teams by the coordinators.
  • Teams have to choose only 1 problem statement by 4th October and have to inform the coordinators about the chosen problem statement on the WhatsApp group.
  • The allocation process will take place on a FIRST COME FIRST SERVE basis. Thereafter that particular statement will no longer be available to the rest of the participants.
  • All development work on the problem statement should be done at the Hackathon venue.
  • Wi-Fi access and power points/Extensions will be available.
  • Teams have to report at the venue at 11:00 AM on 6th October.
  • Refreshments will be provided (evening snack, dinner, next day breakfast,tea,coffee and Biscuits available throughout the night).
    • MorningAfternoonNight

  • All refreshments to be consumed in the pantry
  • The Hackathon will begin at 11:00 AM on 6th October and will end at 11:00 AM morning on 7th October. The teams are free to take breaks in between.
  • The teams will not be provided with any additional accommodation.
  • Teams are not allowed to leave the venue until the end of the hackathon.
  • Each team will have five minutes (without including the time for setting up their websites or apps) to present to the judges in a presentation manner, which will be followed by a two-minute Q&A session by the judges.
  • Presentations and demo will be shown on a projector connected to your system (LAN,HDMI,Wifi)
  • In case of discrepancy, decisions made by the judges will be final.

Judging Criteria

  • Solution to problem (60%)
  • Creativity(15%)
  • Innovation(15%)
  • Presentation(10%)
