Framed

Welcome to "Framed," an exciting photography event that invites you to explore the world through your lens! Whether you're an experienced photographer or just starting your journey, this competition is your canvas to express your creativity. Our goal is to celebrate the power of photography and the stories it can tell. So, pick up your camera, frame the world as you see it, and be part of this incredible photographic journey.

Eligibility

Students from Undergraduate, Diploma from Professional and Non-professional Institutions and Higher Secondary Schools

Rules

  • Participants must submit a total of 3 (three) photos, where each photo completely follows the criteria mentioned.
  • Participants should submit photos in .jpeg, .jpg or .png format.
  • Participants are allowed to use Mobile phones and DSLRs.
  • Photos should be taken between the given time period.
  • Photos should include all the original metadata.
  • Basic editing such as colour enhancement and cropping of the photo is allowed.
  • Advanced editing used to create an illusion, manipulation, filters and adding/removing of
  • significant elements, and watermarks within the frame is prohibited.
  • Participants are required to provide a unique caption for the images submitted.
  • Submit your image files renamed as 'img_1' 'img_2' & 'img_3'.
  • All the details of the photo will be checked.
  • Participants have to submit only original images (Images that are taken by you and not
  • anyone else). In case we confirm any instance of a Participant copying someone else's
  • pictures and submitting them as their own, or otherwise cheating in the competition, will
  • directly be disqualified.
  • Any Participant found submitting pictures taken from the internet, will directly be
  • disqualified.
  • Exceeding the submission time limit will lead to disqualification.
  • Late registration will not be entertained.
  • Use of drones is not allowed.
  • Violation of the instructions will lead to disqualification.
