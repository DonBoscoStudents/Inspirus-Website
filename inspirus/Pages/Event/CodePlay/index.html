Eligibility

  • Undergraduate and Diploma Students from all professional and non-
  • professional institutions as well as students from Higher Secondary Schools
  • Skills required:
  • Coders: Knowledge of C or C++
  • Players: Speed, Hand-eye coordination, Agility, Creativity, Promptness

Rules

  • The students participating should be from the same institution (Interdepartmental
  • teams allowed).
  • A PC with Microsoft Visual Studio installed for C/C++ will be provided to each team.
  • This event will have the teams of 2 participants each divided as coders and players.
  • So each team will comprise of coder and a player.

Round 1

  • Coders tackle smart solutions to tricky problems ,code it and submit.
  • Players dive into mini-games like "minute to win it," testing their reflexes and quick
  • decisions.
  • Coders' coding time and players' mini-game time are combined for a fantastic total.
  • The top 12 teams with the fastest combined time and impressive coding skills make it to
  • the next round.
  • Time: 10 mins for obstacle course, 45 mins for coding.

Round 2

  • The challenge escalates! Now it's about teamwork and coding smarts.
  • The main problem splits into parts hidden behind mini-games.
  • Players unlock parts, coders weave them together into a masterpiece.
  • Code quality, accuracy, and alignment with the problem statement are the scoring stars.
  • The top 5 teams showcasing the most precise codes move forward.
  • Time: 45 mins.

Round 3

  • Partners become a dynamic duo: one codes while the other dives into games.
  • Game wins mean bonus points or extra time for coding, creating a strategic twist.
  • The judge evaluates the final code and adds extra points for brilliance.
  • The top 2 teams emerge as the champions of this thrilling journey.
  • Time: 45 mins.

Tie Breaker

  • If there's a tie, a special round determines the ultimate winner.

Notes

  • Enjoy a 15-min break after each round to recharge.
  • The event spans a total of 3 hours, packed with excitement!
  • This is more than a competition; it's a journey where minds, skills, and teamwork unite for
  • an unforgettable experience.
  • Rounds 2 and 3 depend on the number of participants, any changes will be
  • conveyed to the applicants.

Judging Criteria

  • LOGIC , ACCURACY , INDENTATIONS , READABILITY , IMPLEMENTATION , AND SPEED
