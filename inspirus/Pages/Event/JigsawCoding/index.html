Eligibility

Students from undergraduate, diploma from professional and non-professional institutions and higher secondary schools.

Rules

  • Registration will be through an online form.
  • The participants must be from the same institute (interdepartmental teams are allowed).
  • Participants should have the knowledge of C and C++ programming languages.
  • A total of 3 rounds will be held, round 1 of 30 minutes, round 2 of 50 minutes and round 3 of 70 minutes .

Rounds

    ROUND 1: Code Detective

  • This is a pen and paper round, the participants will be provided with codes for which they have to find the output and errors in it
  • The top 10 teams will be selected for round 2 .
  • The top 10 teams will be selected for round 2 .
  • The top 10 teams will be selected for round 2 .

    • ROUND 2: Code Synergy Showdown

  • The team will be divided into the code pirate and the warrior.
  • The code pirate will be given a specific output and a piece of code wherein the lines of code will be missing.

  • The top 5 teams will be qualified for round 3.

  • Teams will receive a target output and must create code with the fewest possible characters to achieve it.
  • The top 2 teams who complete the round with the most efficient codes will win!!
