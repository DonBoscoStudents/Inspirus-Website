Eligibility

Students from Undergraduate, Diploma from professional and Non-Professional Institutions and Higher Secondary Schools

Rules

  • Registration will be through an online form.
  • The form will be closed on 03/10/2023 OR if the maximum number of participants is reached.
  • No Limit on the number of teams , Multiple Entries allowed.
  • Team Registration: can register and participate as a sole participant or in a team.
  • Theme: Coding Culture and Programming
  • Language used: English
  • Your meme must include any of the following keywords: Engineering, Programming, Runtime error, Bug, Debugging, Hello world, Reference, Error, Libraries, Arduino, Github, Compile time, Hackathon, Stack overflow, Push commit, Front end, Back end , Cookies, fork.
  • Do not use any obscene, political content; keep it family-friendly.
  • The meme should not hurt anyone's religious sentiments.
  • The participant will be responsible for any copyright issues.
