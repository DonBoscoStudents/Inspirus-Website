Eligibility

Students from Undergraduate, Diploma and Higher Secondary Schools from professional non-professional institutions.

Rules

  • Inspiration can be drawn from the existing trends online but plagiarized content will lead to disqualification.
  • Any derogatory remarks, name-calling, and/or regional slang towards any person or community should not be used.
  • The use of offensive and inappropriate language or actions will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • The video should not exceed 1 minute.
  • Participants should submit only one reel, multiple entries will lead to disqualification.
  • The video should be made vertically and not horizontally. The preferred aspect ratio is 16:9.
  • Participants will be given a 3-second video template which has to be included before the reel starts, in which they have to mention their theme and entry number.

Theme

Participants are free to choose any one of the following:

  • Day in my college
  • Colours of Goa
  • Food blog
  • Ganeshutsav 2k23
  • Travel diaries
  • Movie spoofs (participants are free to use actors)
  • Power of AI

  • After successful registration, participants will have to post their reel on their Instagram account (After adding the entry details template ).
