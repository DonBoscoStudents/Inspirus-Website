Eligibility

Undergraduate and Diploma Students from professional and non-professional institutions.

Rules

  • The students participating should be from a college.
  • Replacement of any participant of a team is not allowed after registration.
  • There shall be a limit of 2 teams per college.(First-come-first-serve basis)
  • Maximum 30 teams will be allowed to register after which registrations will be closed.
  • The theme of the quiz will be Tech, Science and general knowledge.
  • In case there is an entry of more than 5 teams, there will be a preliminary round wherein 5 teams will be selected by elimination based on the points.
  • These 5 teams will advance to the main round where the final 2 winning teams will be determined.
  • If the scores are tied for the 1st or 2nd place in the main round then there will be a Tie-breaker round conducted.
  • The rules of each round will be announced prior to it.
  • In case a team leaves the quiz mid-way, they will not be able to compete further.
  • The decision of the quiz masters will be final and will not be subject to any change.
