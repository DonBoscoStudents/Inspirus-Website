Incarnate

Behind the appearances that the physical eyes can see, there is a lot more thinking and imagination which is concrete and lasting. So don't be daunted, dare to express and draw your dreams. We bring to you, a Digital Art Competition: Incarnate! where you will use your creativity and artistic skills over an exciting theme! "Dreams and Imagination" is a theme that delves into the ethereal landscapes of the mind, where thoughts transcend boundaries to create vivid scenarios, unrestrained by reality. It celebrates the transformative power of envisioning the impossible and the beauty of exploring uncharted territories within the boundless realm of creativity.

Eligibility

Students from Undergraduate, Diploma and Higher Secondary Schools from professional non-professional institutions.

Rules

  • Create a captivating digital artwork that captures the essence of 'Dreams and Imagination.'
  • Along with the Artwork, a brief description of the content is also expected.
  • 24 hours will be given to submit the digital art from 10:00 a.m , 6th October 2023 to 10:00 a.m , 7th October 2023.
  • Use of all illustrative software is permitted. However, the use of software with readily available resources is not allowed and will lead to the disqualification of the participant.
  • Plagiarism checks will be made. Any participant found to be plagiarizing will be disqualified.
  • Do not make use of AI. Checks will be made using AI-detectors.
  • The drawing should not contain any lewd content. All such entries will be disqualified.
  • Further details will be given before the event.
  • In case of any disputes or discrepancies, the decision made by the Organizing Committee will be final.

Judging Criteria

  • Create a captivating digital artwork that captures the essence of 'Dreams and Imagination.'
  • Narrative and Storytelling (20 points)
  • Theme (20 points)
  • Proof (20 points)Proof (20 points)
  • Visual Execution(Artistic Technique,Composition,Color and Mood,Attention to Detail,) (20 points)
