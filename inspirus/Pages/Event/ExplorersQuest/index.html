Explorers Quest

Assemble your team and be prepared to dive into a world of riddles, puzzles, ciphers and surprises. This event puts to test your knowledge, spontaneity, capability, trust in others and street skills under the pressure of a relentless ticking clock. As the rounds go by, the hunt gets more intense & frantic! There will be tricks and traps around every corner, unexpected challenges and lots of high-stakes drama. So participants, do you have what it takes to find the treasure and prevail against all odds? Will you be the next Indiana Jones or will you crumble under the pressure?

Eligibility

Students from undergraduate, Diploma from Professional and non-professional Institutions and higher secondary schools

Rules

  • Registration will be through an online form.
  • The form will be closed on date OR if the maximum number of participants is reached.
  • The registration form should be filled out by the team leader only once. Any team submitting more than one form will be disqualified.
  • Only the team leader should join the WhatsApp group via the link provided after the submission of the form.
  • Round-specific instructions will be provided before each round.
  • The event will be made up of 3 rounds.
  • The first and second rounds will be made up of riddles, ciphers, and logical or aptitude questions.
  • Based on the cumulative score of the first two rounds, teams will be eliminated and only the top 5 teams will go to the final round.
  • The final round will require each team to solve each clue and find the key to the treasure.
  • All teams will receive different clues and will be located at different places, with the final clue leading to a key to the treasure.
  • All teams should take the clues along with them.
  • The first 2 teams to find their keys will be awarded 1st and 2nd place accordingly.

Judging Criteria

  • Create a captivating digital artwork that captures the essence of 'Dreams and Imagination.'
  • Narrative and Storytelling (20 points)
  • Theme (20 points)
  • Proof (20 points)Proof (20 points)
  • Visual Execution(Artistic Technique,Composition,Color and Mood,Attention to Detail,) (20 points)
