Cubix

Contestants race against the clock to arrange the cube's colored faces into a uniform pattern, typically aligning all sides to have the same color. This event showcases the speed and dexterity of cubers as they manipulate the cube's complex twists and turns, impressing audiences with their ability to solve it in record times. Whether you're a novice or a seasoned cuber, this event is a captivating display of mental acumen and finger dexterity.
